ZauTempo

Using ZauTempo

  • Start checking tempo by clicking, on BEAT button.
  • Tap BEAT button along with rhythm of the music you are trying to know the tempo.
  • Change combo box in beat display to one of the selection 2/x, 3/x, 4/x to confirm beats.
  • Pressing R button in the beat display to correct the beat timing.
  • Use Reset button to reset.
